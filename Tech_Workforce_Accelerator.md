# Idea: International-Ready Tech Workforce Accelerator

## 1. Title
International-Ready Tech Workforce Accelerator for Iranian Talent

## 2. Author
(Optional)

## 3. Abstract
This idea proposes creating a specialized company that prepares Iranian technical talent (developers, engineers, product managers, designers) to work at an international level. While Iran has a strong pool of skilled professionals, many lack experience with global work standards, communication practices, and collaboration in international teams.

The company would offer intensive, advanced training programs focused on both technical excellence and global readiness — including remote collaboration, English communication, agile workflows, and real-world project experience aligned with international expectations.

## 4. Why It’s Interesting
- Iran has a large, underutilized pool of high-quality technical talent  
- Global demand for remote tech workers continues to grow  
- Market liberalization will expose a gap in international work readiness  

This initiative can:
- Increase employability of Iranian professionals in global markets  
- Reduce brain drain by enabling remote work opportunities  
- Create a new export-oriented service sector (talent export)  
- Help local companies upgrade standards indirectly  

## 5. Approach
Phase 1 (0–3 months): Design
- Define curriculum based on global company standards (engineering, product, design)
- Partner with mentors with international experience
- Build assessment framework (entry and exit benchmarks)

Phase 2 (3–9 months): Pilot Programs
- Launch intensive bootcamps (8–16 weeks)
- Focus areas:
  - Advanced technical skills through real-world projects
  - English for professional and technical communication
  - Agile and Scrum workflows
  - Remote collaboration tools (Git, Jira, Slack)
  - Code review and team collaboration practices

Phase 3 (9–18 months): Scaling
- Partner with international companies for hiring pipelines
- Provide job placement or freelance onboarding support
- Build alumni network and credibility system

Phase 4 (Ongoing): Platform Expansion
- Develop an online learning platform
- Offer certifications recognized by international employers
- Provide continuous upskilling programs

## 6. Help Needed
- Senior engineers with international experience  
- Product managers and agile coaches  
- English instructors specialized in tech communication  
- Partnerships with international companies and startups  
- Initial funding for program development and marketing  

## 7. Supporting Material (recommended)
- Sample curriculum roadmap

- Mock interview frameworks
