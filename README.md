# Future of Iran — Project Ideas

An open, bottom up repository of ideas to help build the future of Iran across tech, infrastructure, economy, and society.

Anyone can contribute.

---

## How to Submit an Idea

Each idea should be **1 to a few pages**, clear and actionable.

### 1. Title
Short, descriptive name.

### 2. Author (optional)
Name, 1–2 line background, contact.

### 3. Abstract
What is the idea, why it matters, what problem it solves.

### 4. Why It’s Interesting
Why now, why Iran, expected impact.

### 5. Approach
Key steps, timeline, technologies or methods.

### 6. Help Needed
Talent, funding, data, partnerships.

### 7. Supporting Material (recommended)
Charts, images, links, videos.

---

## Guidelines

- Be concise and practical  
- Focus on solutions, not politics  
- Prefer clarity over length  
- Visuals strongly help  

---

## Goal

Create a high quality, open set of ideas that can turn into real projects, teams, and companies.
